Research and Articles
Heavy metals in dates (Phoenix dactylifera L.) collected from Medina and Dhaka City markets, and assessment of human health risk
2024· Environmental System Research - Springer
Date fruit is a sacred fruit to muslims and is consumed all around the world, which, if contained excessive concentrations of heavy metals, may pose a major risk to human health. The amounts of heavy metals in different date varieties were measured after they were collected from wholesale markets in the major cities of Dhaka, Bangladesh, and Medina, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. The study uses indices like Average Daily Intake (ADI), Maximum Permissible Limit (MPL), Health Quotient (HQ), and Health Index (HI) to determine the health risk posed by date fruit consumption.
